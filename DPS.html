Delhi Public school, Dharan

Delhi Public School, BPKIHS Dharan, is a Senior Secondary School (XI-XII), affiliated to Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The School is a Coed Day Cum Boarding School, with classes from Pre-nursery to XII. It is an English Medium school. The school is located in BPKIHS area of Dharan. Delhi Public School, BPKIHS was established in 1996. It is a Society and is part of Delhi Public School (DPS) and is managed by The Delhi Public School (DPS) Society.

The motto of Delhi Public School BPKIHS is Service Before Self. It is a Coed Day Cum Boarding School, which services the educational need of over students from Pre-nursery through XII. The classes in Delhi Public School for every academic year starts from April.

The school is the result of an agreement between the Government of Nepal and the Government of India which took the shape of an institution in the year 1996. The school is located within B.P Koirala Institute of Health Sciences in Sunsari District which lies in the eastern part of Nepal.
